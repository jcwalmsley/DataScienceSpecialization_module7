Project grading criteria


1)      Did the report include an executive summary?

2)      Did the student answer the questions of interest or detail why the question(s) is (are) not answerable?

3)      Did the student interpret the coefficients correctly?

4)      Did the student quantify the uncertainty in their conclusions and/or perform an inference correctly?

5)      Did the student do some exploratory data analyses?

6)      Did the student fit multiple models and detail their strategy for model selection?

7)      Did the student do a residual plot and some diagnostics?

8)      Was the report brief (about 2 pages long) for the main body of the report and no longer than 5 with supporting appendix of figures?

9)      Was the report done in Rmd (knitr) with pdf output?
